Q: What if SYNERGOS makes a mistake?
A: All alerts are explainable. You'll see:
   - Which dimension(s) triggered the alert
   - Why (e.g., "intent field shows exfiltration hotspot near credential field")
   - Confidence level (0-100%)

   This lets you triage false positives quickly.

Q: Can attackers evade SYNERGOS?
A: Extremely hard. They'd need to violate laws of physics/game theory:
   - Can't hide intent without exfiltration (where does data go?)

   Evading all 4 dimensions simultaneously is mathematically impossible.

Q: What about privacy?

   - Sketch-based variant uses O(1) memory per site
   - Federated learning shares anonymized statistics (not raw forms)
   - On-device edge computing keeps data local
   - No central database of user interactions
