[1] INTENT FIELD ANALYZER
   Core: Model form behavior as potential energy field where attacker intent
         creates measurable gradients. Malicious forms concentrate "energy"
         at exfiltration endpoints; legitimate forms distribute energy diffusely.
   
   Why Unique: Treats malicious intent as conserved quantity flowing through
               form topology. Detects phase transitions and anomalous gradients
               without knowing specific obfuscation techniques.
   
   Complexity: O(N log N) time | O(N) space (N = form fields)
   Speed: 45ms-1.2s depending on form complexity
   Blind Spot: Distributed intent across many endpoints; high-intent legit forms

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[2] ADVERSARIAL PAYOFF RECONSTRUCTION
   Core: Invert attacker optimization. Observed form design = best response
         to our defense. Reconstruct the payoff function by finding what defense
         strategy would induce the observed form. Detect non-Nash behaviors
         (forms that don't maximize attacker utility) as zero-day signals.
   
   Why Unique: Backward inference catches novel variants before they're in
               databases. Attackers can't hide their objective function—
               deviations from rational optimization reveal new strategies.
   
   Complexity: O(N² · iterations) | O(N²) space (N = strategic features)
   Speed: 50-2000ms depending on feature count
   Blind Spot: Bounded rationality; attackers may copy old non-optimal forms

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[3] BEHAVIORAL PHASE TRANSITION TRACKER
   Core: Model population of attacks as dynamical system. Track order parameter
         measuring obfuscation depth × novelty × recency. Detect phase transitions
         (sharp reorganization of attack population) signaling coordinated evolution.
   
   Why Unique: Population-level detection reveals shared intelligence. Phase
               transitions require coordination; high susceptibility to defense
               changes reveals tightly coupled attacker ecosystem.
   
   Complexity: O(M log M) per window | O(M) space (M = observed forms)
   Speed: 50-100ms per hourly window
   Blind Spot: Isolated, uncoordinated attackers won't trigger transitions

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[4] DECEPTION CASCADE FRAGILITY INDEX
   Core: Map deception tricks as dependency cascade. Measure how much deception
         is lost when each trick is removed (ablation). Identify critical tricks
         and measure cascade redundancy. High fragility = concentrated risk.
   
   Why Unique: Measures robustness of deception strategy. New variants maximize
               fragility (many independent tricks). Old partially-mitigated forms
               have low fragility. Identifies weak links for defense optimization.
   
   Complexity: O(N² + T·M) | O(N²) space (N = tricks, T = ablation tests)
   Speed: 2-4 seconds with full ablation (can approximate in 100-200ms)
   Blind Spot: Trick identification; dependent trick interactions

INTEGRATION WITH SCAMSHIELD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

These algorithms work alongside VERIDICT (existing signature layer):

VERIDICT (Layers 1-3):
  └─ Fast signature matching, conservation law checks, cascade detection
     → High confidence on known patterns

BEHAVIORAL SANDBOX (These algorithms):
  └─ Slower structural reasoning through physics + game theory
     → Catches novel patterns, predicts new variants
     → Feeds evidence to VERIDICT Layer 4 (Immune system)

Deployment Strategy:
  • Phase 1: Intent Field Analyzer (MVP, ~200 lines TS)
  • Phase 2: Adversarial Payoff Reconstruction (game solver, ~300 lines)
  • Phase 3: Phase Transition Tracker (ensemble, ~400 lines)
  • Phase 4: Advanced variants (optimization, research)

Real-Time Budget: <4 seconds (Vercel Edge Runtime)
  • Intent Field: 45ms-1.2s
  • Adversarial Payoff: 50-2s (depending on feature count)
  • Phase Transition: 50-100ms (batch window)
  • Cascade Fragility: 100ms-4s (fast approximate mode)
